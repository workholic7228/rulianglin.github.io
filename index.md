# RuLiang Lin

**Researcher in Systems Security, Trusted Computing, and AI Security**  
Huazhong University of Science and Technology

I am interested in building secure and practical systems for modern computing platforms. My research lies at the intersection of **system security**, **trusted execution environments**, and **secure on-device AI deployment**.

## Biography

I am currently working on topics related to **trusted execution**, **memory isolation**, and **LLM confidentiality protection**. My recent work focuses on how to securely deploy large language models on edge devices and defend them against privileged software attacks.

## Research Topics

- Trusted Execution Environments (TEE)
- ARM Confidential Compute Architecture (CCA)
- OP-TEE and secure monitor interfaces
- On-device LLM inference security
- Model confidentiality protection
- Secure memory management
